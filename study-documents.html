01

Primary Objective

To evaluate median overall survival and progression-free survival of patients with histologically confirmed high-grade gliomas within participating African neuro-surgical units.

02

Secondary Objectives

Assessment of regional variations in surgical resection rates, access to adjuvant chemoradiotherapy, and prognostic markers relevant to African patient populations.

Inclusion Criteria

✓Histologically confirmed WHO Grade 3 or 4 glioma
✓Patients aged 18 years or older
✓Presentation within the last 36 months
✓Availability of post-operative follow-up records
✓Signed informed consent for data inclusion
✓Minimum 3-month survival data post-surgery

Data Collection

A standardized approach ensures data integrity and cross-site compatibility. Collaborators are asked to follow these data guidelines.

Clinical Data

Demographics, symptom duration, and functional status.

Imaging

MRI/CT reports including extent of resection.

Pathology

H&E slides and IHC results where available.

Follow-up

Recurrence dates, adjuvant treatments, and mortality data.

For specialized data entry inquiries, contact the lead epidemiology team at docs@sahara-study.org.
