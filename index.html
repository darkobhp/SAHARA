Survival Analysis of High-grade gliomAs acRoss Africa study

The SAHARA study aims to include African patients with high-grade gliomas, evaluate treatment approaches, compare management across centers, and assess survival outcomes.

Join Study View Sites

Study Aims

Phase I Priority

Primary Aim

To evaluate the survival outcomes for adult patients with high-grade gliomas across African hospitals.

Overall Survival (OS)

Progression-Free Survival (PFS)

Secondary Aims

To identify systemic and regional variations that impact surgical outcomes and patient recovery.

  • ✓Regional Care AnalysisMapping geographical variations in clinical outcomes across the continent.
  • ✓Protocol AdherenceAssessing compliance with adjuvant radiotherapy and chemotherapy standards.
  • ✓Clinical RecoveryMonitoring post-operative complication rates and long-term mortality markers.
